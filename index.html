How Umuhlewena works

Four messages.
A lifelong relationship.

After every visit, a gentle, personalised WhatsApp sequence reminds your client that she is beautiful — and that you remember her.

1
Immediately after visit
The Warm Send-Off
A personalised message that names the treatment, makes her feel seen, and delivers 2–3 relevant aftercare tips. The first impression she gets after leaving your chair.
2
Day 3–5
The Care Message
A genuine check-in. How is her skin? How is her hair? Maintenance tips that feel like a friend, not a business. This is where trust is built.

The Beauty Ritual Reminder
Beauty is a ritual, not a once-off. This message plants the seed of regularity — not as a sales push, but as genuine care for her wellbeing and confidence.

A soft, confident invitation to come back. With a booking link. Grounded in her own wellness routine. This is how a once-off visit becomes a loyal client.
